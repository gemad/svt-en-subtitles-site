FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

On first launch, the extension downloads a ~150MB Opus-MT translation model from Hugging Face's CDN. This is a one-time download — the model is cached in your browser's Cache Storage, so subsequent launches are instantaneous.

Traditional translators send your text to cloud APIs (Google Translate, DeepL, etc.). This extension runs neural inference entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. No subtitle text, no viewing habits, and no personal data ever leaves your device.

The popup UI lets you adjust Swedish and English font sizes independently, swap the text order (Swedish/English vs English/Swedish), toggle between stacked and side-by-side layouts, and reset the overlay position. All preferences persist across sessions.
