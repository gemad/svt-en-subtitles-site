Privacy Policy

Your Data Stays With You

This extension was built with a strict privacy-first philosophy.

🔒

No Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or viewing habits. Period.

💾

Local Storage Only

User preferences (font sizes, layout mode, enabled state) are stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device.

🌐

Network Requests

The only external requests are: (1) a one-time model download from Hugging Face on first use, and (2) fetching VTT subtitle files from SVT's own servers. No other network activity.

🚫

No Third-Party Services

No analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), no crash reporting, no ad networks, no tracking pixels. The extension has zero dependencies on external services for translation.

This privacy policy applies to version 3.1.0 and later of the SVT Play – English Subtitles Chrome extension. For questions, contact us at support@svtensubtitles.com.
